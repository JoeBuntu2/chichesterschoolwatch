Cost Per Student - I propose this as the best metric on which to compare the cost-effectiveness of one district versus another. Simply put, how many dollars does it take to educate each child. You will note, that Chichester is far ahead of the pack. You'll notice that even our spending 4 years ago was more than what districts are currently spending (except for GV, a much, much wealthier district).

If the community has any interest in putting an end to our excessive taxes, I suggest cost-per-student as the number everyone focuses on. Other metrics are just too easily manipulated through smoke and mirrors (such as tax rate increases.)
