You can try going to budget meetings, but you will only be privy to a small sliver of the $79 million budget. The board members have large binders that provide detailed information that contain very useful information such costs over the last several years, cost-per-student compared other districts, etc.

When you ask for a copy of the same budget material the board members have, the administrators will have no problem. The board will say no. This is a picture of the school board denying my PA right-to-know request that was an attempt to get a copy of the information in these large binders.

Board President Ruth Anne Baiocco disputes whether I had access to this information that I was clearly denied access to. She asserts that I had access to the information here at the 12:30 mark of this 5-22-19 board meeting video.
