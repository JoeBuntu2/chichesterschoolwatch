Why Should You Care About Chichester SD Taxes?
Do you know any of the following?
1Chichester spends $4-5k more per-student than other comparable Delco School Districts.
2Chichester would cut $12-17 million dollars in expenditures if it simply kept costs in line with other Delco school districts and schools across PA.
3If those 12-17 million dollars were cut from Chichester's $48 million in local tax revenue, tax-payers would see a 25%-35% reduction in property taxes.
4For a home assessed at $165,000, a 25%-35% reduction in school property tax would reduce a home owners $6500 school tax bill between $1,640 and $2,300.
5Chichester spends $955 more per-student than our much wealthier neighbor district Garnet Valley.
6Chichester put a 3.47 million dollar deficit on the books this year.
7Chichester did not raise taxes this year. This is a joke. Of course you heard about the 0% increase.
