The amount that expenditures exceed revenues on the budget. Put another way, a deficit is when the amount of cash going out exceeds the amount of cash coming in. Unlike tax rate increases, Chichester School Board members do not post these numbers on social media. By silently amassing a {{stats.chiDeficitFy1920 | thousandSuff}} deficit this year, the Chichester School Board was able to keep tax rate increases at 0% this year.
