How much ChichesterSD would save if it's costs were in line with other districts.
2For each district, this shows how much we would cut in costs if our costs-per-student were the same as that district.

For Example: PennDelco spends $5,535 less per student than Chichester. If you multiply this difference in cost-per-student by Chichester's enrollment ( I use average enrollment of 3,247 from the previous 4 years ) you'll end up with almost 18 million in cost savings.
