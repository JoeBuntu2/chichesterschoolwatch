Chichester School Board seems to want the community to believe that state pension contributions are putting an enormous strain on district finances. The truth of the matter is that State contributions to PSERS increases each year. The last two years Chichester's net contribution (Chichester's obligation after the state pays) into PSERS has slightly decreased. That is, PSERS pension funding has had zero contribution to this years 3.47 million deficit or Chichester SD's excessive cost-per-student.
